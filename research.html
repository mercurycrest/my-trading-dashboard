Research Coverage

Valuation Models

Discounted cash flow (DCF) analysis, comparable company analysis (comps), sum-of-the-parts, and EV/EBITDA-based valuations — built from scratch using company filings and market data. Includes price target derivation and sensitivity analysis.

Technical Analysis

Chart structure, trend analysis, key support and resistance levels, momentum indicators, and pattern identification across multiple timeframes. Covers global equities listed on major exchanges including NYSE, NASDAQ, LSE, and others.

Market Commentary

Macro context, sector rotation themes, event-driven analysis, and earnings impact assessments. Tailored commentary on how broader market conditions affect specific holdings or sectors in your portfolio.

Report Types

Initiating Coverage

Earnings Analysis

Post-results breakdown covering beats/misses, guidance changes, margin trends, and revised outlook.

Sector / Thematic

Short-form, time-sensitive commentary on a specific event, announcement, or market development.
